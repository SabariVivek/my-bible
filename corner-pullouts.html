Design 02 — Bottom-Right Arc
Summary

God told Jonah to go to the city of Nineveh and warn the people about their evil ways. But Jonah did not want to obey God. He ran away and got on a ship going in the opposite direction.

While they were sailing, God sent a strong storm. The storm was so bad that the sailors were afraid the ship would break. They prayed to their gods and threw their goods into the sea to make the ship lighter. Jonah was sleeping during all this.

When the sailors found out that Jonah was running away from God, Jonah told them the storm was happening because of him. He told them to throw him into the sea so the storm would stop. At first, they did not want to do it, but finally they threw him overboard. Immediately, the sea became calm.

The sailors then feared the Lord and offered a sacrifice to Him. God sent a big fish to swallow Jonah, and he stayed inside the fish for three days and three nights.
